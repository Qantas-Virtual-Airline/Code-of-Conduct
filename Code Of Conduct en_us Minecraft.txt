# Code-of-Conduct-Minecraft

## QANTAS VIRTUAL  CODE OF CONDUCT FOR MINECRAFT
### ARTICLE I: LEGAL PREAMBLE & STATUS
1.1 Non-Affiliation: Qantas Virtual (QV) is a non-commercial, hobbyist simulation organization. We are NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Qantas Airways Limited. All trademarks and logos are used under "Fair Use" for educational and roleplay purposes.

1.2 Scope: This document governs all interactions on the QV Minecraft Server, Discord, and associated platforms.

1.3 Jurisdictional Compliance:

Australia: Adherence to the Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Consumer Law.

European Union: Compliance with GDPR regarding data portability and erasure.

USA: Adherence to COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

### ARTICLE II: PRIVACY & DATA PROTECTION (GDPR/APP)
2.1 Data Collection: We collect Minecraft UUIDs, IP addresses (for security), and Discord IDs.
2.2 Purpose: Data is used strictly for rank tracking, security (ban-evasion prevention), and safety auditing.
2.3 Data Rights: Any member may request a "Data Audit" to see what information QV holds on them or request a "Permanent Wipe" upon exiting the VA.

### ARTICLE III: CHILD SAFETY & SAFEGUARDING
3.1 The "Golden Rule": QV hosts a diverse age range (13+). Adult members (18+) must maintain professional distance from minors.
3.2 Solicitation: Any attempt by an adult to move a minor to a private, unmoderated chat for non-VA purposes will result in an immediate report to the Australian Centre to Counter Child Exploitation (ACCCE).
3.3 PII Protection: Sharing real-world names, addresses, or school info is strictly prohibited.

### ARTICLE IV: GENERAL CONDUCT & HARASSMENT
4.1 Zero Tolerance: Harassment, Doxing, or Hate Speech regarding race, religion, gender, or orientation results in an immediate permanent blacklist.
4.2 Cyber-Bullying: "Banter" is not an excuse for targeted toxicity. If a member is told to stop, they must stop immediately.
4.3 Brand Protection: You represent Qantas Virtual. Trolling other groups or "Air Rage" in public servers while carrying the QV tag is a punishable offense.

### ARTICLE V: VATSIM INTEGRATION
5.1 VATSIM Compliance: Pilots must follow the VATSIM Code of Conduct. Failure to follow ATC instructions or "Slewing" on the network brings disrepute to QV.
5.2 Cross-Platform Discord: * VATSIM Discord: https://discord.gg/RFCxdrqVav

## VATSIM Discord: https://discord.gg/RFCxdrqVav

### ARTICLE VI: MINECRAFT TECHNICAL LAWS
6.1 Griefing & Theft: Any unauthorized modification of QV assets or stealing from community chests is "Malicious Sabotage."
6.2 Hacking: Use of hacked clients (Wurst, Meteor, etc.) or X-Ray is a Tier 3 offense.
6.3 Server Security: Attempting to bypass MineKeep's protection or "Lag-Machine" the server is a violation of the Computer Misuse Act.

### ARTICLE VII: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
7.1 Asset Ownership: All liveries, builds, and scripts developed for QV remain the property of the QV Development Team.
7.2 Non-Leaking Policy: Sharing internal files or "Early Access" builds outside of QV platforms is strictly prohibited.

### ARTICLE VIII: DISCIPLINARY DUE PROCESS
8.1 Tier 1 (Warning): Minor infractions/First-time professionalism slips.
8.2 Tier 2 (Suspension): Repeated offenses or moderate griefing.
8.3 Tier 3 (Blacklist): Serious breaches (Hacking, Doxing, Safety violations).
8.4 Appeals: Every member has the right to appeal a ban after 30 days via a formal "High Command Review."

### ARTICLE IX: STAFF ETHICS
9.1 Abuse of Power: Staff may not use "Admin" commands for personal gain or to settle personal disputes.
9.2 Transparency: All bans must be logged with evidence (screenshots/logs) for High Command audit.

### ARTICLE X: INDEMNIFICATION & RATIFICATION
10.1 Liability: QV is not responsible for any hardware damage or account loss on 3rd party platforms.
10.2 Agreement: By joining any QV platform, you acknowledge you have read, understood, and agreed to this 10-article Code of Conduct.
